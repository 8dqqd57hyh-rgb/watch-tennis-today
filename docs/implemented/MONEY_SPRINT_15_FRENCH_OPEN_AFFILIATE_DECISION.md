# Money Sprint 15 — French Open Affiliate Decision Layer

## What changed

Added a reusable French Open streaming decision component that gives users a clear monetization path:

1. Find official broadcaster by country
2. Read the Roland Garros VPN guide
3. Click the affiliate VPN deal

## Files changed

- `app/components/FrenchOpenStreamingDecision.tsx`
- `app/french-open/page.tsx`
- `app/where-to-watch-french-open/page.tsx`
- `app/roland-garros-recap/page.tsx`
- `app/french-open-live/page.tsx`

## Why this helps

French Open traffic is high intent but can leak if pages only show schedules/results. This block turns informational traffic into a guided decision flow:

- country broadcaster guide for trust and SEO
- VPN guide for commercial intent
- direct affiliate CTA for monetization

## Safety / compliance

The copy avoids promising bypass access and clearly says users should start with official broadcasters and legal subscriptions.
