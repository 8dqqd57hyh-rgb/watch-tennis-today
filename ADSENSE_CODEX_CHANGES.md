# AdSense compliance changes

This version was updated to better satisfy common AdSense review concerns from the Google help article about rejected AdSense accounts: insufficient content, low-quality/thin content, content policy risk, navigation problems and unclear traffic/user value.

## Changed files

- `app/layout.tsx`
  - Added clearer top navigation links to the legal viewing guide and About page.
  - Added footer links to the new streaming-rights guide and author page.
  - Expanded the footer legal disclaimer to clarify external links, official providers and affiliate partners.

- `app/components/ContentQualityNotice.tsx`
  - Added a reusable editorial notice for dynamic pages.
  - Clarifies that the site is informational and does not host, embed, restream or bypass copyrighted broadcasts.
  - Adds a user-first reminder to verify match times and availability with official providers.

- `app/player/[slug]/page.tsx`
  - Added the editorial/content-quality notice to player pages.
  - Helps reduce the risk that programmatic player pages look like thin or autogenerated pages.

- `app/watch/[slug]/page.tsx`
  - Added the editorial/content-quality notice to match pages.
  - Strengthens the legal-streaming and user-value signals on pages most likely to be reviewed.

- `app/watch-tennis-in/[country]/page.tsx`
  - Added the editorial/content-quality notice to country broadcaster guides.
  - Reinforces that broadcaster information is guidance and should be verified with official sources.

- `app/tennis-streaming-rights-explained/page.tsx`
  - Added a new long-form educational article explaining tennis streaming rights, regional availability, legal viewing checks and safe viewing practices.
  - This adds original informational content, improves internal linking and reduces “low value content” risk.

- `app/sitemap.ts`
  - Added the new streaming-rights article to the sitemap.

## Why these changes help

- More original explanatory text on the site.
- Better navigation to trust, editorial and legal pages.
- Clearer distinction from illegal streaming sites.
- Stronger disclosures for affiliate/external links.
- More useful context on dynamic match, player and country pages.
