# Money Sprint 19: French Open Watch Intent Router

## What changed
- Added a reusable `FrenchOpenWatchIntent` component.
- Added the block to `/french-open`, `/french-open-draw`, and `/french-open-today`.
- The block routes visitors by intent:
  - watch live now
  - see today's schedule
  - find the legal broadcaster by country
  - follow the active draw
- Added a soft affiliate/VPN conversion row for travelers/public Wi-Fi users.

## Why this helps
The French Open pages now push users toward high-value internal pages instead of leaving them to scroll. This improves retention, internal linking, and affiliate conversion opportunities.

## Safety note
The VPN copy stays framed around privacy/travel access to legal subscriptions. The site still does not host or recommend unauthorized streams.
